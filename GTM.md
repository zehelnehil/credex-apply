# Go-To-Market

## Target Personas

Founder-operators at 3 to 50 person startups who added AI tools quickly and now need to make renewal decisions.

Finance or ops leads at seed to Series A companies who do not yet have procurement software but need lightweight vendor discipline.

Agencies and fractional CFOs who can share reports with clients.

## Acquisition

- SEO pages around AI tool cost audits, ChatGPT Team cost, Copilot vs Cursor spend, and startup SaaS renewal checklists
- Founder communities where people ask what AI tools are worth paying for
- Partner distribution through fractional CFOs and startup finance advisors
- Shareable audit URLs that make reports easy to forward internally

## Conversion Loop

1. User runs a free audit.
2. User receives concrete savings recommendations.
3. Report is shareable with a founder, CFO, or team lead.
4. Lead capture appears after value delivery.
5. Credex follows up with renewal cleanup or SaaS spend review.

## Why This Fits Credex

The product creates qualified intent. A user who enters spend data and asks for a renewal plan is much warmer than a generic newsletter signup.
